Document Verification

How It Works?

  • Step 1

    Buyer obtains a copy of the property documents from the seller.

    A complete list of documents that the buyer has to obtain is provided here

  • Step 2

    Buyer chooses Document Verification Service and makes a payment.

    Buyer chooses Document Verfication Service and makes a payment. To know the packages click here.

  • Step 3

    Buyer uploads the scan copies of the documents in our site.

    Buyer uploads the scan copies of the documents.

  • Step 4

    One of our legal experts verifies your documents.

    One of our legal experts verify your documents and let you know whether they are clear or not. If they need any more document, they will request you for the same. They will tell you that you are good to go only if they are convinced.

Documents Required (Content copied from an external site. For demo purpose only)

  1. Absolute sale deed in present seller’s name
  2. Khata certificate and extract from BBMP
  3. Latest tax paid receipt
  4. If any loan outstanding on the property, latest statement from bank
  5. Encumbrance Certificate from date of purchase till date
  6. Agreement of sale and construction executed by developer in favour of seller
  7. Latest electricity bill and receipt for the said flat
  8. NOC from Apartment Association
  9. Sanctioned building plan
  10. Possession/occupancy certificate from builder
  11. All title documents of land owner
  12. Joint development agreement, GPA, and Sharing/supplementary Agreement, between land owner and builder
  13. Whether originals are available for inspection if no loan is taken?
  14. A Copy of all registered previous agreements (in case of re-sale property)
  15. RTC (Records of Rights and Tenancy Corps) or 7/12 extract
  16. Conversion Order issued by the concerned Authority
  17. Registered development agreement (If in case of Joint Development Property)
  18. Power of attorney/s if any
  19. Photocopy of Society share certificate and Society registration certificate.
